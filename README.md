## FAQ

**How do I connect with my partner?**
Open the app, complete registration, and share your couple code with your partner.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

**I forgot my password. What do I do?**
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email.
