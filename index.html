FAQ

How do I connect with my partner?
Open the app, complete registration, and share your couple code with your partner.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → HiLove.

I forgot my password.
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and follow the email instructions.
